The six risk surfaces

Most "DeFi due diligence" stops at TVL and audit firm logos. We score the operational config that actually changes between audits and silently changes risk.

admin_keys

Admin keys & upgradeability

Who holds proxy admin? Is upgrade gated by timelock or instant? How many sigs on the multisig and is it active? Single EOA proxy admins are the highest-impact finding we surface.

oracle_dependency

Oracle dependency

Which oracles feed the contract, what's the fallback, what's the deviation tolerance, and has the oracle source list changed since the last audit. Stale oracle paths are routinely the post-mortem root cause.

verifier_centralization

Verifier centralization

For ZK and rollup-adjacent protocols: who can submit/challenge proofs, how many verifiers are active, and what fraction of the verifier set is correlated (same operator, same hosting, same client).

timelock_window

Timelock window & bypass

Stated timelock vs. actual recent change cadence. Emergency-pause bypass paths. Whether the timelock contract itself can be replaced without going through the timelock.

governance_drift

Governance drift

Voting power concentration, recent quorum trends, last proposal participation rates. Quiet drift toward a single voter is rarely surfaced by the protocol's own dashboards.

audit_recency

Audit recency & coverage gap

Date of last external audit, scope vs. current contract surface, count of contracts deployed after the audit cut-off. Most "audited" protocols have meaningful unaudited surface in production.
