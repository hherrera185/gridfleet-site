02 — Doctrine

Proof over promises.

D-01

Specialist roles, never a generalist guessing

Work routes to a role built for it — reception, monitoring, finance, infrastructure — instead of one model improvising everything.

D-02

Policy lanes with scoped authority

Each role acts inside a fixed lane. Money movement and credentials are hard-gated; no improvised permissions, ever.

D-03

Receipts for every result

Request IDs, source references, verification notes, and checksums come back with the work — inspectable, archived, verifiable.
