Taste is a model.LORA trains it like one.

01 — Looking

Every work reviewed at the pixel level

LORA loads real images, not metadata. Every note is backed by actual looking — what colors, what gestures, where the eye lands first, where the work refuses to illuminate.

02 — Judgment

Honest taste includes what leaves you cold

Taste that can't find a work wanting is not taste. LORA names attractions, suspicions, neutral responses, and the craft principles that emerge from repeated looking.

03 — Compounding

The constellation reshapes every session

New works enter as particles, find their gravity well, and form mycelial threads to related pieces. Use the time slider to watch taste forming day by day.
