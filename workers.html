AI worker products sell a good idea: pick a role, give it a goal, connect tools, and let it work. Grid adds the missing enterprise layer: safe autonomy settings, blocked-action gates, degraded-verification labels, and receipts for every meaningful action.

05 — Receipts timeline

Every action carriesa status, not a story.

Grid labels the truth: verified, posted-unverified, skipped-no-write, blocked, failed, or degraded. If an agent cannot prove a result, the product says so before the customer has to ask.

  • Message IDs, job IDs, tweet IDs, call IDs, hashes, and URLs.
  • Browser/API readbacks after external writes.
  • Human approval references for high-risk actions.
  • Blockers routed to the owning worker instead of buried.

06 — Safe autonomy

Autonomy is a setting,not a slogan.

L0

Chat only

Answers and drafts; no writes.

L1

Internal research

Reads approved sources and prepares reports.

L2

Internal write

Updates files, dashboards, and internal state.

L3

External draft

Prepares external actions for human approval.

L4

Approved execute

Acts in approved channels with readback proof.

L5

High-risk gated

Money, legal, credentials, deploys, calls, and public posts require scoped approval.
