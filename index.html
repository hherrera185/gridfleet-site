The difference

A chatbot answers. GridFleet acts — and shows its work.

Most AI products end at a confident sentence. GridFleet ends at a verified outcome: the right specialist role picks up the job, uses real tools inside a governed lane, and returns request ids, screenshots, source references and checks. You inspect evidence instead of trusting a claim.

How it works

Send a job. The fleet routes it. You inspect the proof.

    Send the job
