Straight answers

The questions owners actually ask.

Is this legal? What about TCPA and consent?

Vera is built TCPA-aware from day one. She never pretends to be human — every call is disclosed as AI-assisted. Outbound SMS is limited to confirmations, reminders, and messages a caller asked for; a consent record is checked before any send, STOP and UNSUBSCRIBE are honored immediately, and messaging is throttled per plan. Vera does no cold outbound calling or texting.

What happens when a caller wants a human?

Vera warm-transfers to your escalation number and gives whoever picks up a spoken handoff summary — who's calling and what they need. If nobody answers, she takes a structured message, and it lands in your inbox attached to the call receipt so nothing gets lost.

What happens off-hours and on weekends?

Vera answers 24/7. Off-hours she books directly into your open calendar slots, answers questions from your knowledge base, and queues anything that needs a human decision into your morning summary — so you open the day with booked jobs instead of voicemails.

What if she doesn't know the answer?

Vera only answers from your ingested knowledge base — your website, documents, and business profile. If the answer isn't there, she says so and offers to take a message or transfer. She refuses to fabricate. Every answer she gives is grounded in something you gave her.

How long does setup take?

About five minutes of your time: point Vera at your website and business profile, connect your Google Calendar, set your escalation number and rules. She learns your services, hours, and pricing from what you already publish, and you review before she goes live.

Do I have to change my phone number?

No. You get a dedicated Vera number and forward your existing line to it (or publish it directly). Turn forwarding off any time and you're back to exactly where you were.
